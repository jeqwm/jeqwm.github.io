About Us

Our Roots

Bridiging Smiles was founded in 2025 by Jesus Quiroz. As an avid volunteer and Certified Dental Assistant, Mr. Quiroz found himself volunteering in countless dental clinics across North Carolina. During his time as a volunteer, he encountered several issue that were common at every clinic: continuity of care, patient education, and knowledgeable translators. These issues prompted Mr. Quiroz to have a conversation with providers and clinic directors to come up with a solution to these issues. What resulted was Bridging Smiles.

Our Team

Jesus Quiroz, Founder

Jesus Quiroz is the founder of Bridging Smiles. He is currently an undergraduate at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. He became a Cetified Dental Assistant in 2021 and has worked over 4 years as a general dental assistant with experience as a pediatric dental assistant. Mr. Qurioz hopes to pursue his dreams to become a General Dentist, serving the rural communitites of North Carolina. Outside of school, Mr. Quiroz is heavily involved with the Newman Catholic Student Parish serving as a lector, Catechesis Instructor, Small Faith Group (SFG Leader), Newmen's Ministry Team, and by playing the Cajon with the CCN band. Mr. Quiroz enjoys spending time with friends, working out, reading books, practicing Muay Thai and Boxing, swimming, learning new skills like sewing, and volunteering at dental clinics.
